How do I like a reply in a course announcement in Announcements Redesign as an instructor
Open Announcements
In Course Navigation, click the Announcements link.
Open Announcement
Click the name of the announcement.
Like Announcement Reply
Locate the reply and click the Like icon.
Like Threaded Announcement Reply
To view threaded replies, click the # of replies link. 
To like a threaded reply, click the reply's Like icon. 
View Liked Announcement Reply
The Like icon fills in, indicating you have liked the reply.
The Like icon also displays the number of times a reply has been liked.
