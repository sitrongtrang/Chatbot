How do I use inline chat as a student
Open Course
In Global Navigation, click the Courses link and click the name of your course.
Open Chat
View inline chat in your course. To open your chat, click the Expand icon. Chat must be reopened whenever you navigate to a new page within your course.
View Chat
Chat messages appear in the content window.
Send Message
To send a chat message, enter your message in the chat window. Then click the Send button.
Hide Chat
To hide the chat window, click the Collapse icon.
