What is New Quizzes
Instructors
Build Quizzes
Instructors can use New Quizzes to build quizzes with several item types that measure student competency in a variety of subjects. Quizzes can be modified using various quiz settings and aligned to outcomes at the quiz or question level.
Moderate Student Attempts
Instructors can moderate student attempts and review students' answers.
View Reports
New Quizzes includes quiz reports for admins and instructors to view student performance, including mastery of learning outcomes.
Students
Complete Quizzes
Students can access New Quizzes from a Canvas course to take a quiz.
View Quiz Results
Students can view their results from New Quizzes quizzes. Quiz results include overall and individual questions scores. They may also include instructor feedback.
