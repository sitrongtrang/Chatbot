Updating a Canvas quiz
Open Quizzes
In Course Navigation, click the Quizzes link.
Open Quiz
Click the title of the quiz you need to moderate.
Moderate Quiz
To moderate the quiz, click the Moderate This Quiz link. On the Moderate Quiz page, you can give the student an extra attempt.
Ask Student to Submit Quiz
Contact the student who is currently taking the quiz and ask them to submit the quiz. If they haven't finished the quiz, they will need to confirm their incomplete submission.
Prevent Quiz Access
To prevent students from accessing quizzes before they are complete, you can: 
Leave the quiz unpublished in the course until complete, or place the quiz in an unpublished module. 
Use availability dates in quiz settings to prevent students from accessing the quiz until a specific date.
