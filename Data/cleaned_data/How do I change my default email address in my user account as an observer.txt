How do I change my default email address in my user account as an observer
Open User Settings
In Global Navigation, click the Account link, then click the Settings link.
View Email Addresses
The User Settings sidebar displays your contact options. View your email address contacts under Email Addresses.  Your default email address displays a Star icon. 
Choose Default Email Address
To select a new default email address, hover over the preferred default email address and click the Star icon.
