How do I close a discussion for comments in a group as a student
Open Discussions
In Group Navigation, click the Discussions link. 
View Discussions
The group Discussions Index page is very similar to the course Discussions Index page. It displays a link to the discussion and indicates whether the user is subscribed. A green discussion icon indicates a subscription to the discussion.
Close for Comments
Click the Options icon and select the Close for comments link.
View Discussion
The discussion is listed on the Discussions Index page in the Closed for Comments section.
