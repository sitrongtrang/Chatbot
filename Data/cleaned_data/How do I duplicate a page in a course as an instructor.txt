How do I duplicate a page in a course as an instructor
Open Pages
In Course Navigation, click the Pages link.
View Pages
Pages is designed to open to the front page for the course, if there is a front page selected. To select a page from the Pages Index, click the View All Pages button.
Open Page
Locate the page you want to duplicate. Click the Options icon, then click the Duplicate button.
View Duplicate
View the page duplicate in your course.
Note: If applicable to your course, Blueprint Course icons display after the page is refreshed.
