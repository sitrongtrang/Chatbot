How do I add an account-level role in the Permissions page as an admin
Open Account
In Global Navigation, click the Admin link, then click the name of the account.
Open Permissions
In Account Navigation, click the Permissions link.
Open Account Roles
Click the Account Roles tab.
Add Role 
Click the Add Role button.
Add Role Name
In the Role Name field, type the name of the new role.
Save Role
Click the Save button.
View Account Role
View the new account-level role.
