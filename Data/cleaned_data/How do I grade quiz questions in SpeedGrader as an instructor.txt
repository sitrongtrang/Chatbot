How do I grade quiz questions in SpeedGrader as an instructor
Open Quizzes
In Course Navigation, click Quizzes.
Open SpeedGrader
In the Quizzes page, click the Options icon of the quiz you want to grade. Then click the SpeedGrader link. 
View Question
To view a question, scroll down the quiz to locate the quiz question you want to view. 
If a question needs to be manually graded, you can click the question number directly within the notification window. 
Assign Score
In the points field, enter the score for the question.
Assign Grade
You may also enter fudge points to adjust a student's score for the entire quiz. To assign a quiz score, click the Update Scores button.
