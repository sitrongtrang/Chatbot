What is the Canvas Student app
View Courses and Groups
Students can access their courses and groups from the Dashboard. 
View Course or Group Navigation
Courses and groups include the same navigation items that can be viewed in the browser version of Canvas.
View Assignments
Students can view details on assignments, quizzes, and discussions and submit these items from the app.
Navigate App
Students can use the Navigation Bar to access their dashboard, calendar, To Do list, notifications, or Inbox.
Manage Account
Students can manage their Canvas account using the Student app. Students can manage their files, display course grades, get help from Canvas Support, change users, and log out of the app.
