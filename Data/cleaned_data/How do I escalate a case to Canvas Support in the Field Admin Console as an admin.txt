How do I escalate a case to Canvas Support in the Field Admin Console as an admin
Open Case
Click the Cases tab. Then click the case number link for the case you want to escalate.
Open Escalate Tab
Click the Escalate tab.
Escalate Case
Click the Escalate button.
Select Queue
In the Do you want to escalate this case to the L1 or L2 Queue drop-down menu, select the L1 or L2 queue.
Then click the Next button.
