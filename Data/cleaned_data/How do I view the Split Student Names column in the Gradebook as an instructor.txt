How do I view the Split Student Names column in the Gradebook as an instructor
Open Grades
In Course Navigation, click the Grades link.
Split Student Names
Click the Settings icon. 
To separate the names, click the View Options tab and click the Split Student Names checkbox. Click the Apply Settings button.
View Split Student Names
View the separated last and first student names. 
Combine Separated Names
Click the Settings icon. 
To combine the separated columns, click the View Options tab and deselect the Split Student Names checkbox. Click the Apply Settings button.
