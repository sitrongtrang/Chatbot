How do I submit a Lucid document for a Lucid assignment as a student
Open Assignments
In Course Navigation, click the Assignments link.
Open Assignment
Click the title of the assignment.
Open Lucid
Complete the assignment using Lucidchart or Lucidspark.
Note: If this is your first time accessing Lucid from Canvas in this course, you may be required to answer questions about your role and age before connecting to your account. You may also be required to set up and connect to a free Lucid account.
Submit Assignment
To submit the assignment, click the Submit Assignment button.
Add Submission Comments
To add submission comments, use the Comments field. 
To submit your comments and assignment, click the Submit button. 
View Submission in Canvas
View the confirmation of your assignment submission in Canvas.
