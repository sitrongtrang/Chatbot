How do I hide totals in my students' grade summaries as an instructor
Open Settings
In Course Navigation, click the Settings link.
Open Course Details
Click the Course Details tab.
Hide Totals in Student Grades Summary
Select the Hide totals in student grades summary option.
Update Course Details
Click the Update Course Details button. 
Students View Informational Message
When students view their course grades, an informational message indicates that the calculation of totals has been disabled. Assignment group names are displayed, but totals are hidden.
