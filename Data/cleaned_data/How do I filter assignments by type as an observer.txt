How do I filter assignments by type as an observer
Open Assignments
In Course Navigation, click the Assignments link.
Group Assignments by Type
Click the Show By Type button.
View Assignments by Type
View course assignments by assignment group.
If the instructor has chosen to weight students' final grades, you can view assignment group weights. Additionally, if the instructor has selected grading rules for an assignment group, you can view grading rules. To view grading rule details, hover over the rule.
