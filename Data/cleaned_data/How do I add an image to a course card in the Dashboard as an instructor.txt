How do I add an image to a course card in the Dashboard as an instructor
Open Course Settings
In Course Navigation, click the Settings link.
Open Course Details
Click the Course Details tab.
Choose Image
Click the Choose Image button.
Upload File
To upload your own image, drag and drop your image into the Upload Image section. Or, to browse your computer and locate an image, click the Upload Image section. 
Images will automatically upload, but larger images may take a few seconds to process. The image will be centered and resized to fit the course card.
View Image
View the image as it will be shown in the Dashboard course card.
Manage Image
To manage the image, click the Options icon. To change the image and select a new one, click the Change image option. To remove the image completely, click the Remove image option.
Update Course Details
Click the Update Course Details button.
