How do I view a QR code to log in to the Canvas mobile apps as an observer
Open QR Code
In Global Navigation, click the Account link, then click the QR for Mobile Login link.
Note: If you cannot view the QR for Mobile Login link, your student's school has not enabled this feature. 
Confirm QR Code Display
To generate your mobile login QR code, click the Proceed button.
View QR Code
View your QR code. The QR code can be scanned from the login page of the Parent app from an Android device or an iOS device.
Note: Mobile login QR codes expire 10 minutes after they are generated.
