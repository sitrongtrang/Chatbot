How do I delete pages in a group as a student
Open Pages
In Group Navigation, click the Pages link.
View Pages
Pages is designed to open to the front page for a group, if there is a front page selected. Click the View All Pages button to select a page from the Pages Index.
Delete Pages
Select the checkbox of the page or pages you want to delete. Then click the Delete button. 
Note: You will be unable to delete a page if it is used as the Front Page for your course.
Confirm Deletion
To confirm deletion of the selected pages, click the Delete button.
