How do I preview a quiz in New Quizzes as an instructor
Open Quizzes
In Course Navigation, click the Quizzes link.
Note: You can also access quizzes from the Assignments page.
Open Build Page
To preview a quiz, find the quiz you want to open.
To open New Quizzes from an existing quiz, click the Options icon and then click the Build link.
Preview Quiz
To preview the quiz, click the Preview button.
View Error Message
If you are currently editing the quiz, you cannot open Quiz Preview.
View Quiz Preview
When previewing a quiz, the quiz displays a colored Preview border. You can enter answer to any question type.
The preview displays most settings, including calculators, timer, question shuffling, and one question at a time. Quiz Preview does not support displaying student access codes, IP address filtering, and multiple attempts.
Submit Quiz
To submit the quiz, click the Submit button.
To exit the quiz and return to the area in Canvas where the quiz was opened, click the Return button.
View Results Page
If you submit a quiz while in preview mode, Quiz Preview will open to the results page. The items and points displayed in the results page corresponds to the quiz's settings to restrict student result view. 
Exit Preview
To exit Quiz Preview, click the Close icon.
