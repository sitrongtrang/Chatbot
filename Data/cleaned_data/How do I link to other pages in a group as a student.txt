How do I link to other pages in a group as a student
Open Pages
In Group Navigation, click the Pages link.
View Pages
Pages is designed to open to the front page for a group, if there is a front page selected. Click the View All Pages button to select a page from the Pages Index.
Open Page
Click the title of the page you want to edit.
Edit Page
Click the Edit button.
Select Link Location
In the Rich Content Editor, place your cursor where you wish to insert the link. 
Insert Link
You can add links to group content. The name of the content will appear in the Rich Content Editor and flash yellow. Then the name will turn blue, indicating it is a link.
Save Changes
Click the Save button.
View Page
View the page.
