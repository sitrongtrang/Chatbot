How do I edit or remove a course outcome group in Improved Outcomes Management as an instructor
Open Outcomes
In Course Navigation, click the Outcomes link.
Select Outcome Group
Click the name of the outcome group you want to modify.
Edit Outcome Group
To edit the outcome group, click the Options icon and click the Edit link.
Edit Group Details
For the outcome group, you can edit the name and description.
To save your changes, click the Save button.
Remove Outcome Group
To remove an outcome group, click the Options icon and click the Remove link.
Note: Removing an outcome group will also remove all outcomes in that group from your course.
Confirm Removal
To confirm you want to remove the group, click the Remove Group button.
