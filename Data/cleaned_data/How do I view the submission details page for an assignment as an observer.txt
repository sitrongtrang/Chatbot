How do I view the submission details page for an assignment as an observer
Open Grades
In Course Navigation, click the Grades link. 
Open Assignment Submission Details
On the grades page, locate the assignment you want to view, then click the assignment name. 
View Submission Details Page
The submission details page displays the submission date and time. 
If the instructor has graded the assignment, you can view the assignment score. You can also view the rubric for the assignment. 
The submission section displays the student's submission. If the student submitted a file upload, you can download and view the submission. You may also be able to view the instructor's annotated feedback in the assignment. 
The comments sidebar displays instructor and student comments.
