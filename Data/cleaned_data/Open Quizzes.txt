Open Quizzes
Open Quizzes
In Course Navigation, click the Quizzes link.
Open Quiz Options
Locate the quiz you wish to send, then click the quiz Options icon. Select the Send To... option.
Select Recipient
To send your quiz to another instructor, click or type in the Send to field. Then click the name of the instructor to receive your quiz. 
Note: You can send the quiz to multiple instructors at one time. 
View Recipients
You can view all selected recipients in the Send to field. To remove a recipient, click the Remove icon.
Note: To share the quiz to yourself, add your name to the Send to field.
Send Quiz
Click the Send button. 
View Sent Notification
Canvas displays a notification when content sends successfully.
View Shared Content
When an instructor receives shared content, a badge displays on the users's Account icon and in the Shared Content link.
