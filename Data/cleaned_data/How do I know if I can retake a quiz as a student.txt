How do I know if I can retake a quiz as a student
Open Quizzes
In Course Navigation, click the Quizzes link.
Open Quiz
Click the quiz title to open the quiz you want to retake.
View Quiz
If you can retake the quiz, you will see Take the Quiz Again button. Click the button to retake the quiz. 
Contact your instructor if you have problems accessing the quiz.
Note: You can see past attempts in your quiz results.
