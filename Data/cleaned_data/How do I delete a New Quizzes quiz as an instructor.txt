How do I delete a New Quizzes quiz as an instructor
Open Quizzes
In Course Navigation, click the Quizzes link.
Note: You can also access quizzes from the Assignments page.
Delete Quiz
Find the quiz you want to delete.
To delete the quiz, click the Options icon and click the Delete option.
Confirm Deletion
To delete the quiz, click the OK button. To edit your quiz in another manner, click the Cancel button.
