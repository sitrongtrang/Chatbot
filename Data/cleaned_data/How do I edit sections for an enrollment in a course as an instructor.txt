How do I edit sections for an enrollment in a course as an instructor
Open People
In Course Navigation, click the People link.
Locate User
In the search field, search for the name of the user. You can also filter users by role in the Roles drop-down menu.
Edit Sections
Click the user's Options icon, then select the Edit Sections link.
View Section Enrollment
Canvas will show you the section(s) where the user is already enrolled.
Add Section
To add a section, begin typing the name of the section in the text field and click the section when it appears.
Note: Users can be in more than one section. You do not need to remove the original section.
Remove Section Enrollment
To remove a student from a section, click the Remove icon.
Update Enrollment
To update section enrollments, click the Update button.
