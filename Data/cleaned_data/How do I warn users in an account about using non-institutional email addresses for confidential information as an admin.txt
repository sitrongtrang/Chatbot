How do I warn users in an account about using non-institutional email addresses for confidential information as an admin
Open Account
In Global Navigation, click the Admin link, then click the name of the account.
Open Account Settings
In Account Navigation, click the Settings link.
Open Notifications
Click the Notifications tab.
Select Display Warning
Select the Display one time pop-up warning on Notification Settings page checkbox.
Update Settings
Click the Update Settings button.
