How do I resize the Rich Content Editor as an instructor
Open Rich Content Editor
Open the Rich Content Editor. You can access the Rich Content Editor in Announcements, Assignments, Discussions, Pages, Quizzes, or Syllabus.
Resize Window
To resize the window manually, click and hold the Resize icon. Then drag the Rich Content Editor window to the desired size.
View in Fullscreen
To expand the Rich Content Editor to fullscreen view, click the Fullscreen icon. You can also click the View menu and select the Fullscreen option.
Exit Fullscreen
To exit fullscreen view, click the View menu and select the Fullscreen option. You can also exit fullscreen by pressing the Esc key on your keyboard.
