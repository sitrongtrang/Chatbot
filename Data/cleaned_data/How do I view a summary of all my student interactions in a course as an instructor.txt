How do I view a summary of all my student interactions in a course as an instructor
Open Grades
In the Dashboard sidebar, click the View Grades button.
Open Student Interactions Report
Locate the course and click the Student Interactions Report link.
Open People
You can also access the report in People. In Course Navigation, click the People link.
Open Student Interactions Report
In the Options drop-down menu, click the Student Interactions Report link.
View Student Interactions Report
The Student Interactions Report lists the students in your course, the last time you interacted with them, the current grade for a student, the final grade for a student, and if there are any submitted but ungraded assignments for the student. You can also send a message to a student by clicking the message icon. To sort the columns, click the arrows at the top of the columns.
