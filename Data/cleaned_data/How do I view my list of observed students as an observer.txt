How do I view my list of observed students as an observer
Open Account
In Global Navigation, click the Account link. 
Open Observing
In the User Account menu, click the Observing link. 
View Observing
The Observing page displays a list of students you are observing. 
You can also link additional students to your observer account using a pairing code. 
Remove Student
To stop observing a student, click the Remove link.
Confirm Removal
To confirm you want to remove the student from your observer list, click the OK button.
