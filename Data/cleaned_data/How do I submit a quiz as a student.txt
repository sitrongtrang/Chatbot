How do I submit a quiz as a student
Open Quizzes
In Course Navigation, click the Quizzes link.
Open Quiz
Click the quiz title to open quiz.
Take Quiz
Click the Take the Quiz button.
Resume Quiz
If you navigate away from the quiz, when you return to the quiz the button will show as Resume Quiz.
Submit Quiz
Answer the questions in the quiz. Learn how to view the quiz page.
When you are finished, click the Submit Quiz button.
Unanswered Questions Warning
If you have forgotten to answer some of the quiz questions, Canvas will confirm your submission before submitting the quiz. To submit the quiz anyway, click the OK button. To return to the quiz, click the Cancel button.
View Results
View the quiz results to find out your score.
