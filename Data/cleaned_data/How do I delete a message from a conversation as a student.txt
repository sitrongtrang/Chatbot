How do I delete a message from a conversation as a student
Open Inbox
In Global Navigation, click the Inbox link.
Select Conversation
Select the conversation. 
Select Message
Click the Options icon and then click the Delete option.
Note: You can only select one message to delete at a time.
Confirm Deletion
Click the OK button to delete the message. 
Note: You can only delete your copy of the message. You cannot delete the message for all recipients.
