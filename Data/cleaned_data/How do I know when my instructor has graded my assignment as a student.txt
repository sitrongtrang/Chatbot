How do I know when my instructor has graded my assignment as a student
Open Grades
In Course Navigation, click the Grades link.
View Assignment Grade
A dot next to the assignment indicates the assignment that has been graded. The indicator disappears when you navigate away or refresh the page.
The score column displays the score given to the assignment.
In the Grades page, you can also see if the assignment includes scoring details, comments, or rubrics.
