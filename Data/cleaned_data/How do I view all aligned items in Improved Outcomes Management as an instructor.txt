How do I view all aligned items in Improved Outcomes Management as an instructor
Open Outcomes
In Course Navigation, click the Outcomes link.
Open Alignments
Click the Alignments tab.
Open Outcome
In the All Outcomes drop-down menu, you can view all outcomes, outcomes with alignments or outcomes without alignments.
Click the expand arrow next to the outcome you want to view.
View Alignments
View the course content items aligned to the outcome and the module in which they are located, if applicable. 
Aligned items are identified with an icon displaying the item type: assignment or rubric. 
You can view the number of course content items aligned to an outcome.
