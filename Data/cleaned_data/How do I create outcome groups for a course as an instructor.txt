How do I create outcome groups for a course as an instructor
Open Outcomes
In Course Navigation, click the Outcomes link.
Add Outcome Group
Click the New Group button.
Create Outcome Group
Type a Learning Outcome group name in the Name this group field. Type a description in the Rich Content Editor. Click the Save button.
View Outcome Group
View the Outcome group. You can create new outcomes within the group by creating a new outcome. You can also move outcomes and outcome groups into the new group.
