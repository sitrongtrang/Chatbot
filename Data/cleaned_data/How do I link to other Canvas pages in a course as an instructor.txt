How do I link to other Canvas pages in a course as an instructor
Open Pages
In Course Navigation, click the Pages link.
View Pages
Pages is designed to open to the front page for the course, if there is a front page selected. To select a page from the Pages Index, click the View All Pages button.
Open Page
Click the title of the page you want to open.
Edit Page
Click the Edit button.
Insert Link
In the Rich Content Editor, place your cursor where you want to insert the link. You can add links to course or group content. The name of the page will appear in the Rich Content Editor and flash yellow. Then the name will turn blue, indicating it is a link.
Save Page
Click the Save button.
View Page
View the page.
