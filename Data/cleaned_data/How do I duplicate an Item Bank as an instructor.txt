How do I duplicate an Item Bank as an instructor
Open Item Banks
In Course Navigation, click the Item Banks link.
Note: You can also access Item Banks from the New Quizzes Build page.
Duplicate Item Bank
To duplicate an item bank, click the Duplicate icon.
Confirm Duplication
To confirm duplication, click the Duplicate button.
View Duplicated Item Bank
Duplicated item banks display in the All Banks filter. Duplicated item banks include "Copy" at the end of the bank name.
