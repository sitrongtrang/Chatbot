How do I star a conversation as an instructor
Open Inbox
In Global Navigation, click the Inbox link.
Star and Unstar Conversations
To create a starred message, click the star icon. If you want to remove the star, click the star icon again. 
View Starred Conversations
To view only starred conversations, click the Inbox drop-down menu. In the drop-down menu, select the Starred link.
View Starred Conversations Only
View all the conversations you have starred.
