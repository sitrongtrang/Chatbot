How do I accept an invitation to join a course as an observer
Receive Email Invitation
You will receive the course invitation via email. The invitation will include the course name and your user role in the course. To complete registration, click the Get Started button.
Note: You will have to sign in to Canvas to accept the course invitation. If you don't have a Canvas account you'll be able to create one as part of the course invitation.
Complete Registration
If you need to set up an account, you will need to create a password, enter your time zone, and view the privacy policy. Then click the Register button.
