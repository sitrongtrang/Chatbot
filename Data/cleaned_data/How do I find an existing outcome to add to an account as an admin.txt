How do I find an existing outcome to add to an account as an admin
Open Account
In Global Navigation, click the Admin link, then click the name of the account.
Open Outcomes
In Account or Sub-Account Navigation, click the Outcomes link.
Find Outcome
Click the Find button. You will find existing outcomes from Account, State or Common Core Standards.
Import Outcome
Select the name of an outcome group to view available outcomes. You can also view outcome groups within other outcome groups. 
When you have located an outcome, click the name of the outcome you want to import. Click the Import button.
Note: You can also import an entire outcome group. 
View Outcome
View the imported Outcomes.
Outcomes and outcome groups are imported to the main outcome level; learn how to move outcomes or outcome groups.
