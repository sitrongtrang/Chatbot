How do I resend student invitations to a course as an instructor
Open People
In Course Navigation, click the People link.
View Pending Enrollments
User enrollments display as pending until the course invitation is accepted.
Resend Individual Invitation
To resend an invitation, locate the user's name and click the Options icon, then select the Resend Invitation link.
Resend All Invitations
To resend all pending invitations, click the Resend link.
Note: The Resend link does not display until at least 24 hours after a user received the course invitation.
