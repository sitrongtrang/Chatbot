How do I apply scores to ungraded assignments as an Instructor as an instructor
Open Grades
In Course Navigation, click the Grades link.
Open Apply Score to Ungraded
Hover over the Assignment group column header and click the Optionsicon.
To apply a score to ungraded submissions and artifacts, click theApply Score to Ungraded link.
Add Apply Score to Ungraded
To apply a certain grade for ungraded submissions and artifacts, enter the grade percentage in the Grade for ungraded artifactsfield. If you would like to apply the missing status, click the Apply missing statuscheckbox.
You can choose to apply the grade to Only ungraded artifacts that are past due or All ungraded artifacts.
Click the Apply Scorebutton.
View Applied Scores
To view the applied grades, you must reload the page.
