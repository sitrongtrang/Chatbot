How do I remove students from a Scheduler appointment in a course calendar as an instructor
Open Calendar
In Global Navigation, click the Calendar link.
Manage Appointment Slot
Find and click the appointment slot.
Remove Attendees
If you want to retain the appointment time slot but need to remove the specific student, click the Remove icon.
Confirm Removal
Type the reason for deletion in the Reason for deletion field. Click the Delete button.
Delete Time Slot
If you need to remove the entire time slot, click the Delete button.
Confirm Deletion
Click the Delete button.
