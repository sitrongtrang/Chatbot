How do I create an anonymously graded quiz using New Quizzes as an instructor
Open Quizzes
In Course Navigation, click the Quizzes link. 
Note: You can also access quizzes from the Assignments page.
Add Quiz
Click the Add Quiz button.
Select Quiz Engine
If your course has New Quizzes enabled, you must select a quiz engine.
To select New Quizzes, click the New Quizzes option.
Then click the Submit button.
Select Anonymous Grading
Click the Graders cannot view student names checkbox. 
Note: Once changes are saved you cannot remove anonymous grading.
Save Assignment
If you want to notify users about any future assignment changes, click the Notify users that this content has changed checkbox.
To save your quiz details, click the Save button. To save and publish your quiz, click the Save & Publish button.
To open the New Quizzes build page and add questions and content to your quiz, click the Build button.
Note: Once changes are saved you cannot remove anonymous grading.
