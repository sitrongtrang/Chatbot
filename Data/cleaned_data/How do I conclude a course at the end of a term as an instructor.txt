How do I conclude a course at the end of a term as an instructor
Open Settings
In Course Navigation, click the Settings link.
Conclude This Course
To conclude your course, click the Conclude this Course link.
Conclude Course
Click the Conclude Course button.
View Confirmation
Verify that the course has been concluded. 
Note: Admins can unconclude courses if necessary. If you are an instructor, please contact your admin for assistance.
