How do I move a student into a different group as an instructor
Open People
In Course Navigation, click the People link.
Open Group Set
Click the name of the group set.
Move Student via Drag and Drop
Expand the group where you want to move the student. Click the student's name and drag it to another group. 
Move Student via Move To Option
You can also click the student name Options menu and select the Move To... link. 
Move Student
In the Move Student sidebar, click the Place drop-down menu. Select the group where you want to move the student.
Click the Move button.
View Groups
Verify the student has been moved to a different group.
