How do I delete a file or folder as an instructor
Open Files
In Course Navigation, click the Files link.
Delete File
To delete a file, click the line item for the file. Click the Deleteicon. Or click the Options icon and then click the Delete link. 
Delete Folder
To delete a folder, click the line item for the folder. Click the Delete icon. Or click the Options icon and then click the Delete link.
Confirm Delete
Click the OK button.
