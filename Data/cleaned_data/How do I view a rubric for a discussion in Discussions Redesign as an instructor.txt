How do I view a rubric for a discussion in Discussions Redesign as an instructor
Open Discussions
In Course Navigation, click the Discussions link.
Open Discussion
Click the name of the discussion.
Show Rubric
Click the Options icon and select the Show Rubric link.
Note: If a rubric has not been added to a discussion, the Options menu includes a Add Rubric link.
View Rubric
View the rubric.
To edit the rubric, click the Edit icon. To find an existing rubric to replace the current rubric, click the Find icon. To remove the rubric from the discussion, click the Delete icon.
