How do I hide grade distribution scoring details from students as an instructor
Open Settings
In Course Navigation, click the Settings link.
Open Course Details
Click the Course Details tab.
Hide Grade Distribution
Check the Hide grade distribution graphs from students checkbox.
Update Course Details
Click the Update Course Details button.
Student View
When the grade distribution graph is hidden, students can view their score, but cannot view any other aspect of the graph, including the high, low, and mean scores.
