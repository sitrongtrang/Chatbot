How do I create outcome groups for an account in Improved Outcomes Management as an admin
Open Account
In Global Navigation, click the Admin link, then click the name of the account.
Open Outcomes
In Account Navigation, click the Outcomes link.
View Outcome Groups
You can create an outcome group within your account. You can nest outcome groups within other groups for improved organization.
To view outcome groups nested within other groups, click the name of an outcome group.
To create a new group, click the Create New Group link.
Create Outcome Group
To create a new outcome group, enter a group name in the Group Name field. Then click the Confirm button.
To reset the Group Name field, click the Clear button.
