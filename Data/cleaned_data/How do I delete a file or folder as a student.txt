How do I delete a file or folder as a student
Delete File
In user or group files, click the line item for the file. Click the Delete icon. Or click the Options icon and then click the Delete link. 
Delete Folder
In user or group files, click the line item for the folder. Click the Delete icon. Or click the Options icon and then click the Delete link.
Confirm Deletion
Click the OK button.
View Warning Message
Files that have been submitted as part of an assignment cannot be deleted.
