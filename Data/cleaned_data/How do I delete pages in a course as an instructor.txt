How do I delete pages in a course as an instructor
Open Pages
In Course Navigation, click the Pages link.
View Pages
Pages is designed to open to the front page for the course, if there is a front page selected. To select a page from the Pages Index, click the View All Pages button.
Delete Pages
Select the checkbox of the page or pages you want to delete. Then click the Delete button. 
Note: You will be unable to delete a page if it is used as the Front Page for your course.
Confirm Deletion
To confirm deletion of the selected pages, click the Delete button.
