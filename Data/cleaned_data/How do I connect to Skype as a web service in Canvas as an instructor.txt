How do I connect to Skype as a web service in Canvas as an instructor
Open User Settings
In Global Navigation, click the Account link, then click the Settings link.
Register Skype
In the Other Services section, click the Skype button.
Log in to Skype
Enter your Skype name in the Skype name field. Click the Save Skype Name button.
Skype will now appear under the Registered Services heading in your Canvas settings.
