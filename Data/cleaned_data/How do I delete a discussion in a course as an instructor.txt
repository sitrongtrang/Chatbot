How do I delete a discussion in a course as an instructor
Open Discussions
In Course Navigation, click the Discussions link. There are two ways to delete a discussion topic. 
Delete from Discussions Page
To delete a discussion from the index page, locate the discussion. Click the Options icon and click the Delete link. 
Delete from Individual Discussion
To delete a discussion individually, click the title of the discussion.
Delete Discussion 
Click the Options icon, then click the Delete link.
Delete Discussion in Discussions Redesign
To delete a discussion in the Discussions Redesign interface, click the Options icon and click the Delete option.
Confirm Deletion
Click the OK button.
