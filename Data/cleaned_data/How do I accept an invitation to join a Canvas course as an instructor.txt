How do I accept an invitation to join a Canvas course as an instructor
Accept Course Invitation
If you've enrolled in or been invited to a Canvas course, you will receive an email that contains your institution's Canvas URL and your email address to use for your login. To accept the course invitation, click the Get Started button.
Accept Course
Canvas will take you to your course and ask you to accept the enrollment. Click the Accept button.
Create Canvas Account
Click the Create My Account button. 
Note: If you already have a Canvas account, click the I Have a Canvas Account button. You'll be directed to the Canvas login page.
Complete Registration
Create a password and enter your time zone. Depending on your account type, you may be asked if you want to receive information, news, and tips from Instructure. 
Select the Acceptable Use Policy checkbox, then click the Register button.
