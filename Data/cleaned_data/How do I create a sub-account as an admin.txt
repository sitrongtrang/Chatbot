How do I create a sub-account as an admin
Open Account
In Global Navigation, click the Admin link, then click the name of the account.
Open Sub-Accounts
In Account Navigation, click the Sub-Accounts link.
Show Accounts
Click the down arrow to show sub-accounts.
Add Sub-Account
Click the Add button.
Title Sub-Account
Enter a title for the sub-account in the title field. Press Return (on a MAC keyboard ) or Enter (on a PC keyboard).
View Sub-Account
View the created sub-account.
