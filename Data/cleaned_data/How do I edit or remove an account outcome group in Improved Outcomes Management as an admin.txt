How do I edit or remove an account outcome group in Improved Outcomes Management as an admin
Open Account
In Global Navigation, click the Admin link, then click the name of the account.
Open Outcomes
In Account Navigation, click the Outcomes link.
Select Outcome Group
Click the name of the outcome group you want to modify.
Edit Outcome Group
To edit the outcome group, click the Options icon and click the Edit link.
Edit Group Details
You can edit the name and description for the outcome group.
To save your changes, click the Save button.
Remove Outcome Group
To remove an outcome group, click the Options icon and click the Remove link.
Note: Removing an outcome group will also remove all outcomes in that group from your account and any courses that imported the outcome group.
Confirm Removal
To confirm you want to remove the group, click the Remove Group button.
