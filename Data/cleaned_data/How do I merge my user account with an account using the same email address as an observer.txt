How do I merge my user account with an account using the same email address as an observer
Open Account Settings
In Global Navigation, click the Account link, then click the Settings link.
Merge User Accounts
To merge user accounts, locate the email associated with the second account and click the Merge icon. 
Confirm Account Merger
Click the Combine button.
Enter Login Credentials
Enter the email address and password for the second account.
View Confirmation Message
Confirm the merge was successful.
