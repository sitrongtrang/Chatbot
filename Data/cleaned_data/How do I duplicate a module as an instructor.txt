How do I duplicate a module as an instructor
Open Modules
In Course Navigation, click the Modules link.
Duplicate Module
Locate the module you want to duplicate and click the Options icon. Click the Duplicate link.
Note:If a module cannot be duplicated, the Duplicate link will not appear in the Options menu.
View Duplicate
View the duplicate module in the Modules Index Page.
Note: If applicable to your course, the Modules page displays any Blueprint Course or Sync to SIS icons after the page is refreshed.
