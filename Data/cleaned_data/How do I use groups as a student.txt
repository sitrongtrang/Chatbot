How do I use groups as a student
Open Group
In Global Navigation, click the Groups link, then click a group name link. 
You can learn more about viewing your groups or viewing all available groups.
View Group Details
You can view the group's course code or nickname and the group name.
The Group Home page displays recent messages, To Do items that are coming up, and a link to the group calendar.
Use Group Navigation
You can use the Group Navigation to view or make group announcements, create a group page, view group membership, create or reply to group discussions, view and upload group files, access group conferences, or access group collaborations.
