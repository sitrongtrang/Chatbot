How do I forward a message from a conversation as an instructor
Open Inbox
In Global Navigation, click the Inbox link.
Select Conversation
Select the conversation. 
Select Message
Click the Options icon and then click the Forward option.
Note: You can only forward one message at a time.
View Compose Window
In the compose message window, you can:
Add recipients
Type a message
Attach files or media
Click the Send button
Note: You cannot change the course or the subject line if you are forwarding a message.
