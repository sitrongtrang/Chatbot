How do I like a reply in a course announcement as an instructor
Open Announcements
In Course Navigation, click the Announcements link.
Open Announcement
Click the name of the announcement.
Like Announcement Reply
Locate the reply and click the Like icon.
View Liked Reply
The Like icon will be highlighted, indicating you have liked the reply. 
When a reply is liked, the Like icon will also display the total number of likes for all users in the announcement.
