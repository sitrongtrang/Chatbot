How do I view registered services for all users in a course as an instructor
Open People
In Course Navigation, click the People link.
Open Registered Services
In the Options drop-down menu, click the View Registered Services link.
View Registered Services
View registered services for users in your course. The page displays each service and which specific members of your course have registered the service. The sidebar shows a summary of the registered service types.
To return to the People page, click the Back to Roster button.
