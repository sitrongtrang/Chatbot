How do I send a page to another instructor as an instructor
Open Pages
In Course Navigation, click the Pages link.
View All Pages
Pages is designed to open to the front page for the course, if there is a front page selected. To select a page from the Pages Index, click the View All Pages button.
Open Page Options
Locate the page you wish to send to a user, then click the page Options icon. Select the Send to... option.
Select Recipient
To send your page to another instructor, click or type in the Send to field. Then click the name of the instructor to receive your page. 
Note: You can send the page to multiple instructors at one time. 
View Recipients
You can view all selected recipients in the Send to field. To remove a recipient, click the Remove icon.
Note: To share the page to yourself, add your name to the Send to field.
Send Page
Click the Send button. 
View Sent Notification
Canvas displays a notification when content sends successfully.
View Shared Content
When an instructor receives shared content, a badge displays on the users's Account icon and in the Shared Content link.
