How do I know if I can retake a quiz in New Quizzes as a student
Open Quizzes
In Course Navigation, click the Quizzes link.
Open Quiz
To open a quiz, click the title of the quiz.
View Attempts
View the number of attempts you have taken. To retake a quiz, click the Begin button.
Note: If the quiz displays differently, your instructor may have used the Classic Quizzes tool to create the quiz. For help retaking a classic quiz, see How do I know if I can retake a quiz?
If you have no attempts remaining, a warning message will appear on the quiz page.
