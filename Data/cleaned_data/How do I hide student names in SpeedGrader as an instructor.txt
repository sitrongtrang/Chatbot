How do I hide student names in SpeedGrader as an instructor
Open SpeedGrader
Open SpeedGrader from any assignment, graded discussion, or quiz.
Open SpeedGrader Options
Click the Settings icon. In the settings drop-down menu, click the Options link.
Hide Student Names
To hide the student names, click the Hide student names in the SpeedGrader checkbox.
Note: If the checkbox to hide student names is not visible in SpeedGrader settings, anonymous grading has been enabled for the assignment. 
Save Settings
Click the Save Settings button.
View Student Names
View the student drop-down menu and verify that all student names are hidden. Student names display as generic names such as Student 1, Student 2, etc.
