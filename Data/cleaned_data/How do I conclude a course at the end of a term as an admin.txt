How do I conclude a course at the end of a term as an admin
Open Account
In Global Navigation, click the Admin link, then click the name of the account.
Open Courses
In Course Navigation, click the Courses link. 
Note: When you open an account, the account defaults to the Courses page.
Find Course
To find the course in the account, use the filter and search options.
Open Settings
In the courses list, click the course Settings icon.
Conclude This Course
To conclude your course, click the Conclude this Course button.
Conclude Course
Click the Conclude Course button.
View Confirmation
Verify that the course has been concluded.
