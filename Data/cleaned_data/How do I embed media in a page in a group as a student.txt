How do I embed media in a page in a group as a student
Open Pages
In Group Navigation, click the Pages link.
View Pages
Pages is designed to open to the front page for a group, if there is a front page selected. Click the View All Pages button to select a page from the Pages Index.
Open Page
Click the title of the page you want to view.
Edit Page
Click the Edit button.
Open Insert Media Tool
Click the Media icon. You can upload and embed media files from your computer, external sources, or Canvas.
Note: Media will be inserted at the location of your cursor in the Rich Content Editor.
Preview Media
Preview your media in the Rich Content Editor.
Save Changes
To save changes to the page, click the Save button.
