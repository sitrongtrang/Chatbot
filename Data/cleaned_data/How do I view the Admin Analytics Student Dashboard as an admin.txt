How do I view the Admin Analytics Student Dashboard as an admin
Open Account
In Global Navigation, click the Admin link, then click the name of the account.
Open Admin Analytics
In Account Navigation, click the Admin Analytics link.
Open Student Dashboard
To open the Student Dashboard, click the Student link.
View Student Dashboard
In the Student Dashboard, you can view the following quick statistics and charts:
Students with a current course score less than 60%
Average grade for enrolled students
Students with no course activity in last 30 days
Average days since the last course interaction
Grade averages for enrolled students
Student submissions by status
Student details
Learn more about viewing and managing statistic and chart data.
Note: All statistics and charts display data based on the selected filters.
