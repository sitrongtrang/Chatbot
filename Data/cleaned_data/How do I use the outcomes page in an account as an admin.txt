How do I use the outcomes page in an account as an admin
Open Account
In Global Navigation, click the Admin link, then click the name of the account.
Open Outcomes
In Account Navigation, click the Outcomes link.
View Outcomes
On the outcomes page, you can create a new outcome, create a new outcome group, and find an outcome.
If you have permission, you can import an outcome.
To view details about an outcome group, click the name of the outcome group. 
To view an outcome, click the name of the outcome.
View Outcome
To view an outcome, click the name of the outcome.
You can view the outcome description, criterion ratings, points needed for mastery, the calculation method, and the calculation method explanation.
To view items aligned with the outcome and outcome artifacts, click the outcome title.
Note: If mastery scales and calculation methods do not display for your outcome, your institution has enabled account-wide and/or course-wide mastery scales and calculation methods.
Manage Outcome and Outcome Group
You can move, edit, or delete an outcome or outcome group.
Notes: 
You cannot delete an outcome that has been used to assess students.
If a rubric includes an outcome that has been used to assess a submission, edits made to the outcome are not reflected in previous rubric assessments.
