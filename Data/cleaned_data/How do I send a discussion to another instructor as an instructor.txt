How do I send a discussion to another instructor as an instructor
Open Discussions
In Course Navigation, click the Discussions link.
Open Discussion Options
Locate the discussion you wish to send, then click the discussion Options icon. Select the Send To... option.
Select Recipient
To send your assignment to another instructor, click or type in the Send to field. Then click the name of the instructor to receive your assignment. 
Note: You can send the assignment to multiple instructors at one time. 
View Recipients
You can view all selected recipients in the Send to field. To remove a recipient, click the Remove icon.
Note: To share the discussion to yourself, add your name to the Send to field.
Send Discussion
Click the Send button. 
View Sent Notification
Canvas displays a notification when content sends successfully.
View Shared Content
When an instructor receives shared content, a badge displays on the users's Account icon and in the Shared Content link.
