How do I duplicate a module item as an instructor
Open Modules
in Course Navigation, click the Modules link.
Duplicate Module Item
Locate the module item you want to duplicate and click the Options icon. Click the Duplicate link.
Note: If a module item cannot be duplicated, the Duplicate link will not display in the options menu.
View Duplicate
View the duplicate module item in your course.
Note: If applicable to your course, the Modules page displays any Blueprint Course or Sync to SIS icons after the page is refreshed.
