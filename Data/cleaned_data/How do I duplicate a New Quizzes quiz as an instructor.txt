How do I duplicate a New Quizzes quiz as an instructor
Open Quizzes
In Course Navigation, click the Quizzes link.
Note: You can also access quizzes from the Assignments page. 
Duplicate Quiz
Locate the New Quizzes quiz you want to duplicate and click the Options icon. Click the Duplicate link.
View Duplicate
View the quiz duplicate in your course.
Note: If applicable to your course, the Quizzes page displays any Blueprint Course or Sync to SIS icons after the page is refreshed.
