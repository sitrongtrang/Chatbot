How do I create outcome groups for an account as an admin
Open Account
In Global Navigation, click the Admin link, then click the name of the account.
Open Outcomes
In Account Navigation, click the Outcomes link.
Add Outcome Group
Click the New Group button.
Note: If the New Outcome button does not display, your institution may be using Improved Outcomes Management.
Create Outcome Group
Type a Learning Outcome group name in the Name this group field. Type a description in the Rich Content Editor. Click the Save button.
View Outcome Group
View the Outcome group. You can create new outcomes within the group by creating a new outcome. You can also move outcomes and outcome groups into the new group.
