How do I merge my user account with an account using the same email address as an instructor
Open User Settings
In Global Navigation, click the Account link, then click the Settings link.
Combine User Accounts
To merge user accounts, click the Merge icon in the Ways to Contact sidebar.
Confirm Account Merger
To confirm that you would like to merge accounts, click the Combine button.
Enter Credentials
Enter the email address and password for the second account.
View Confirmation Message
Confirm the merge was successful.
