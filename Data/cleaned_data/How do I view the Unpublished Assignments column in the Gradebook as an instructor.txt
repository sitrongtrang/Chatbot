How do I view the Unpublished Assignments column in the Gradebook as an instructor
Open Grades
In Course Navigation, click the Grades link.
Show Unpublished Assignments
Click the Settings icon. 
To show the unpublished assignments column, click the View Options tab and click the Unpublished Assignments checkbox. Click the Apply Settings button.
View Unpublished Assignment
View the unpublished assignment column. You can view the assignment name, but the cells are grayed out and are not editable until the assignment is published. 
Hide Unpublished Assignments
Click the Settings icon. 
To hide the unpublished assignments column, click the View Options tab and click the Unpublished Assignments checkbox. Click the Apply Settings button.
