How do I enable Equella for an account as an admin
Open Account
Click the Admin link, then click the name of the account.
Open Settings
In Account Navigation, click the Settings link. 
Enable Equella
On the account settings page, under the features section, select the Equella checkbox. 
Enter Equella Settings
In the Equella Settings section that appears, enter your Equella Endpoint URL.
Update Settings
Click the Update Settings button.
