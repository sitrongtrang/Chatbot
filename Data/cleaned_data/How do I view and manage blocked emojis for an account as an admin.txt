How do I view and manage blocked emojis for an account as an admin
Open Account
In Global Navigation, click the Admin link, then click the name of the account.
Open Settings
In Account Navigation, click the Settings link.
Managed Blocked Emojis
Blocked account emojis display in the Blocked Emojis field. To block additional emojis for all account users, click the Emoji icon.
Select Emoji
To locate an emoji to block, type a keyword the Search field. To block an emoji, click the emoji.
Update Settings
To save your changes, click the Update Settings button.
