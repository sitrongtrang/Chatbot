How do I link a student to an observer in a course as an instructor
Open People
In Course Navigation, click the People link.
Locate Observer
In the search field, search for the name of the observer. You can also filter by observer role in the Roles drop-down menu.
Link to Students
Click the observer's Options icon, then select the Link to Students link.
Link Student
In the name field, type the name of the student you want to link to the observer. Select the full name when it appears.
You can link additional students to the observer, if necessary.
Remove Students
To remove a student, click the remove icon next to the student. 
Update Link
Click the Update button.
View Observer
View the students linked to the observer.
