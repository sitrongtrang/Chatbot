How do I like a reply in a course announcement as an observer
Open Announcements
In Course Navigation, click the Announcements link.
Open Announcement
Click the name of the announcement.
Like Announcement Reply
Locate the reply and click the Like icon.
View Liked Reply
The Like icon displays highlighted, indicating you have liked the reply. 
When a reply is liked, the Like icon also displays the total number of likes for all users.
View Liking Restriction
If your instructor has restricted the liking option to only users with grading permissions, you cannot like your favorite replies. However, you can still view the total number of likes given to each reply.
