How do I subscribe to the Announcements RSS feed as an instructor
Open Announcements
In Course Navigation, click the Announcements link.
Open External Feeds
Click the External Feeds link.
Open RSS Feed
Click the RSS Feed link.
Notes:
If you use a Firefox browser to access Canvas, you can add a Live Bookmark from this screen.
If you use a Safari browser to access Canvas, Safari automatically creates a RSS feed for you from this screen.
Copy URL
Copy the URL in your browser's address bar. Then open your preferred RSS feed reader and paste the copied URL in the text box.
