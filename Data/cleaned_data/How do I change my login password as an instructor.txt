How do I change my login password as an instructor
Open User Settings
In Global Navigation, click the Account link, then click the Settings link.
Edit Settings
Click the Edit Settings button.
Change Password 
Click the Change Password checkbox to create a new password. Type your old password in the Old Password field. Type your new password in the New Password field. Type your new password again in the Confirm Password field.
Note: If you do not have access to change your password, please contact your institution. Your institution will tell you how to change your password.
Update Settings
Click the Update Settings button.
