How do I add captions to new or uploaded videos in the Rich Content Editor in Canvas as an instructor
Open Video Options
Click your newly recorded media or uploaded media. Then click the Video Options link. 
Select Language and File
To specify the language for your captions, click the Closed Captions/Subtitles drop-down menu.
To attach your caption file, click the Choose caption file button. You can upload SRT or VTT files.
Open File
Select your caption file, then click the Open button. 
View Selected File
View your caption file. 
To attach additional caption files, click the Add button. 
When finished adding media and caption files, click the Done button.
Note: If the video has been copied from another course and includes captions, a tooltip displays noting that inherited captions cannot be edited.
Save Changes
Click the Save button.
Notes: 
The assignment, discussion, pages, and quizzes details pages display a Save & Publish button. 
The Syllabus page displays an Update Syllabus button. 
Discussion replies display a Post Reply button.
View Media with Captions
When you add captions to a media file, the file displays a Captions icon.
